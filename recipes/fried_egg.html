Fried Egg

Description

A simple fried egg my way.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • oil that resists high heat (Olive, Avocado, Canola, etc)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • other condiments (siracha, ketchup, etc)

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium high heat
  2. Add oil until warm
  3. Crack egg into pan, letting it rest until solid
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Flip it like a table, in a pushing manner, then pulling and pushing it up
  6. Add condiments and enjoy!
