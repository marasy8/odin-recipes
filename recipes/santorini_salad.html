Santorini Salada

Description

This is a salad from Opa which I tried to replicate! High in fibre and tastes great and couldn't find recipes elsewhere on it.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas (soaked)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • capers
  • fresh parsley
  • greek dressing (vinagrette, lemon juice, dill, etc.)

Steps

  1. Dice up all the ingredients and put together in a bowl
  2. Mix all the ingredients together and just enjoy! A few capers livens it up quite a bit.
  3. Enjoy :)
